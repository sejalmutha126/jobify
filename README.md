# Jobify – AI-Powered Job Search Platform

## Overview

Jobify is an AI-powered job search platform that simplifies the job application process by bringing together job discovery, resume analysis, and personalized recruiter outreach in a single platform.

It helps applicants find relevant opportunities, evaluate how well their resume matches a job description, identify missing skills, and generate personalized cold emails to improve their chances of getting noticed.

---

## Features

- 🔍 Search and discover real-time job listings
- 📄 Upload and analyze resumes against job descriptions
- 🎯 AI-powered resume matching and ATS compatibility score
- 🧠 Identify missing skills and receive improvement suggestions
- ✉️ Generate personalized cold emails for recruiters
- 📌 Save and organize job opportunities
- 📊 Track application progress in one place
- 🔒 Secure authentication and user profiles

---

## How It Works

1. Create an account and upload your resume.
2. Search for jobs based on your interests.
3. Browse live job listings.
4. Compare your resume with any job description.
5. View your match score and improvement suggestions.
6. Generate a personalized cold email for the recruiter.
7. Apply with a stronger, optimized profile.

---

## Why Jobify?

Applying for jobs often requires searching across multiple platforms, tailoring resumes, and writing outreach emails manually. Jobify brings these tasks together into a single workflow, making the application process faster, more organized, and more effective.
